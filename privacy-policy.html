Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Century Roof & Solar ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our SMS notification program and business operations.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Job site address and project details
  • Communication preferences
  • Information provided during service agreements or onboarding

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Sending SMS notifications related to your roofing or solar project (scheduling, status updates, crew arrival)
  • Internal field reporting and crew coordination
  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Completing contracted services

SMS Messaging Program

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages from Century Roof & Solar related to your project. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any SMS message. To request help, reply HELP.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may be shared only with service providers who assist in delivering our services, and only as necessary to complete your project.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
