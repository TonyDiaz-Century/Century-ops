Overview

Century Roof & Solar ("we," "our," or "the Company") is committed to protecting the privacy of our employees and customers. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our internal operations management system and SMS notification program.

This policy applies to the Century Ops platform and all SMS communications sent by Century Roof & Solar.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Employee information: Full name, phone number, job role, and crew assignment — provided during onboarding.
  • Customer information: Name, address, phone number, and email — collected during the service contracting process.
  • Job data: Job site addresses, job types, scheduling dates, and contract values.
  • Field reports: Progress updates and photos submitted by field crew via the field report form.
SMS Notifications & Consent

Century Roof & Solar operates an internal SMS notification program to communicate job assignments, schedule updates, and field report reminders to our field employees.

How consent is obtained: Employees provide their mobile phone number to the Company during onboarding and verbally consent to receive SMS notifications related to their work assignments. Customers may provide their phone number during service contracting for job status updates.

Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on scheduling activity and job assignments.

Message and data rates: Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-out: You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

Help: Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly at the information below.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for internal business operations, including:

  • Scheduling and assigning field crew to job sites
  • Sending job assignment and schedule SMS notifications to employees
  • Tracking job progress and field reports
  • Customer communication regarding their roofing, solar, or electrical project
  • Internal revenue and operations reporting
Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Mobile phone numbers and SMS consent information are never shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

We may share information only as required by law, or with service providers who assist in our operations (such as SMS delivery platforms) under strict confidentiality agreements.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Our operations platform is password-protected and access is restricted to authorized Century Roof & Solar personnel.
