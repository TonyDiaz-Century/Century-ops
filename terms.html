Program Description

Century Roof & Solar (LIC #721502, C10 & C39) operates an internal SMS notification program called Century Ops. This program sends automated SMS text messages to field employees and, where applicable, customers regarding:

  • Daily job assignments and crew scheduling
  • Schedule changes or updates
  • Field report reminders
  • Job status notifications

This program is operated solely for internal business communications and is not used for marketing or promotional messaging.

Consent to Receive Messages

By providing your mobile phone number to Century Roof & Solar during employee onboarding or customer service contracting, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Century Roof & Solar related to job assignments, scheduling, and operational updates.

Consent is not a condition of employment or purchase of services.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on job scheduling activity. Employees actively assigned to jobs may receive daily messages. Messages are sent only during normal business hours.

Message & Data Rates
Message and data rates may apply. Standard SMS and data rates charged by your mobile carrier may apply to messages you receive from Century Roof & Solar. Contact your mobile carrier for details about your plan.
Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of SMS messages from Century Roof & Solar at any time.

To stop receiving messages: Reply STOP to any message from us. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent to your number.

To get help: Reply HELP to any message or contact us directly at tonyd@centuryrooftile.com.
No Third-Party Sharing

Mobile phone numbers and SMS opt-in data will not be shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes under any circumstances.

Information may be shared only as required by law or with operational service providers (such as SMS delivery platforms) under strict confidentiality agreements.

Supported Carriers

SMS messages are supported by all major US carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and others. Carrier support may vary.

Modifications

Century Roof & Solar reserves the right to modify these Terms & Conditions at any time. Continued receipt of messages after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
